ALL ABOUT TORNADOS

Tornadoes are powerful, rapidly rotating columns of air that extend
from a thunderstorm to the ground. They typically appear as a funnel-shaped cloud extending downward from
the base of a cumulonimbus cloud, although not all tornadoes are visible
due to weather conditions or terrain. Tornadoes are one of the most violent and destructive
weather phenomena, capable of causing widespread devastation in a matter of minutes.
